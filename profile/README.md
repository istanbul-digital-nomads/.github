# Istanbul Digital Nomads 🇹🇷

**A community for remote workers, freelancers, and digital nomads living in or visiting Istanbul.**

Istanbul is one of the world's most exciting cities for digital nomads: affordable living, fast internet, incredible food, and a culture that blends East and West. This community brings together people who work remotely from Istanbul to share tips, organize meetups, and help each other thrive.

## What We Do

- **Weekly meetups** at cafes and coworking spaces across the city
- **Resource sharing** for housing, visas, coworking, and local life
- **Skill exchange** and collaboration between nomads
- **Social events** to explore Istanbul together
