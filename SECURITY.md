# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in any Istanbul Digital Nomads project, please report it responsibly.

**Email:** security@istanbulnomads.com

Please include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Any suggested fixes

## Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgment:** Within 48 hours
- **Initial assessment:** Within 1 week
- **Resolution target:** Within 30 days for critical issues

## Scope

This policy applies to all repositories in the [istanbul-digital-nomads](https://github.com/istanbul-digital-nomads) organization.

## Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Issues in third-party dependencies (report upstream)

## Recognition

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will credit reporters in our changelog when a fix is released, unless anonymity is preferred.
